UI/UX Enhancements – Core Design Initiative

Leadership Highlight: Building Unified BI Design Standards & Modern Visual Frameworks

As part of my leadership role in the UI/UX Core Team, I spearheaded an initiative to redefine the visual and interactive experience of enterprise BI dashboards. The goal was to achieve consistency, accessibility, and a polished, modern aesthetic across Power BI reports.

  • Established a unified Power BI design framework with brand-consistent colors and typography.
  • Developed reusable UI templates for reports to accelerate development and ensure quality.
  • Enhanced user accessibility through better contrast, navigation, and layout balance.
  • Led collaboration between designers and BI developers to embed usability principles into technical workflows.
  • Improved adoption by creating sleek, intuitive dashboards tailored to business decision-making.
